Place your MP3 sound files here. The game will run silently without them.

Required files:
  launch.mp3      - Sound when interceptor fires (short rocket whoosh)
  explosion.mp3   - Sound on successful intercept (boom)
  hit_city.mp3    - Sound when Iranian missile hits city (thud/impact)
  game_over.mp3   - Game over sting (short dramatic sound)
  level_up.mp3    - Wave increase alert (short alert/beep)

Free sources:
  https://freesound.org  (search: missile launch, explosion, rocket)
  https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/game-sound-effects/
  https://opengameart.org/content/512-sound-effects-8-bit-style

Tip: Keep files under 200KB each for fast loading.
